Case Study

Urban Motion
Campaign

Client Meridian Apparel Co.
Category Brand Campaign
Year 2024
Scope Creative Direction, Production, Photography

The Challenge

Meridian Apparel Co. wanted to reposition their brand from mid-market streetwear into the premium lifestyle space — without losing the cultural credibility that made them relevant in the first place. The challenge was to speak fluently across two audiences that rarely share the same visual language.

We built the campaign around the idea of motion as identity — the way the city moves, and how clothing moves through it. Shot across five New York neighborhoods over three weeks, the imagery captures authentic energy while achieving the elevated production standard the new positioning demanded.

The Result

The campaign launched across digital, print, and out-of-home placements simultaneously. Within the first 30 days, Meridian saw a 42% increase in site traffic and their highest recorded sell-through rate on a seasonal collection launch.

The work was featured in Hypebeast and Highsnobiety, cementing Meridian's repositioned identity and opening doors to wholesale partnerships with premium retailers that had previously declined.
